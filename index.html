About CGPA Calculator

What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is a numerical representation of a student's overall academic performance across all completed courses. It is calculated by dividing the total grade points earned by the total credit hours attempted.

How CGPA is Calculated

The CGPA calculation follows these steps:

  • For each course, multiply the grade point by the credit units
  • Sum all the grade points × credit units values
  • Sum all the credit units
  • Divide the total grade points by the total credit units

Formula: CGPA = Σ(Grade Point × Credit Units) / Σ(Credit Units)

How This Calculator Helps Students

  • Academic Planning: Estimate future performance by calculating different scenarios
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your academic performance throughout your studies
  • Goal Setting: Determine what grades you need to achieve your target CGPA
  • Time Management: Identify which courses require more attention based on credit weight
  • Easy Experimentation: Try "what-if" scenarios without manual calculations

Tips for Academic Success

  • Regularly calculate your CGPA to stay on track with your academic goals
  • Focus more on courses with higher credit units as they impact your CGPA more
  • Use this calculator to plan what grades you need in future courses
  • Remember that consistent performance is key to maintaining a good CGPA
